# **App Name**: GymTrack Pro

## Core Features:

- Comprehensive Dashboard: Get a real-time overview of your gym's most important metrics right from the main dashboard.
- Expiring Soon List: A dedicated table on the dashboard shows you exactly which members' subscriptions are about to expire, helping you to follow up with them for renewals.
- Member Management: A complete system to manage your members. Add, edit, and view member details. Assign and manage membership plans. Track member attendance.
- Trainer Management: Manage your gym's trainers and their profiles.
- Payment and Invoice Management: Handle your gym's finances with an integrated system for payments and invoicing.
- Public Member Check-in: A unique Gym ID allows your members to check in at a public-facing page, streamlining the attendance process.
- Secure Authentication: User accounts are secured with Firebase Authentication, ensuring that only authorized users can access the dashboard.
- Plan Management: Create and manage different membership plans with varying prices and durations.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Emerald green (#36D399) to represent growth and vitality within the fitness context.
- Background color: Dark slate gray (#4A5568) to provide a professional, dark-themed aesthetic.
- Accent color: Teal (#008080) complements the primary Emerald color, offering visual contrast to call to action items or highlights.
- Body and headline font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) for a modern and readable interface, ensuring legibility on gym reception tablets and desktops.
- Responsive grid layout for dashboard metric cards, optimized for tablets at gym reception.
- Use a set of consistent, minimalist icons related to fitness and gym activities, in a light color, for quick recognition of elements and to reinforce a modern aesthetic.
